Pattern 07 - Break the grid

Bento layout - different sizes, one rhythm

Pattern 08 - Make them feel the difference

Drag-to-compare: WhatsApp chaos vs Bhraman

An interactive slider the visitor drags to reveal the old way vs your way. Instead of telling them booking is broken, you let them wipe from chaos to calm with their own hand. Physical, memorable, and it dramatizes your whole value prop in one gesture. Drag the handle.

Pattern 09 - Social proof in motion

Operator marquee - the roster, always moving

A slow infinite marquee of verified operators. Signals scale and trust ("look how many real teams are here") without a static wall of logos. Hover pauses it. Feed from live verified operators.

Nine patterns total across both files. Pick 3–4 signature ones - the sticky scrollytelling (06) and drag-compare (08) are the highest-impact for a trust story. Don't ship all nine; a page alive with a few perfect moments beats one screaming with all of them.
