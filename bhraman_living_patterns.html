Pattern 01 - Participant, not reader

The interactive vibe selector

Instead of a static "AI planner" search box, the hero itself responds. Tap a vibe - the image, the headline, and the whole mood shift live. The visitor is doing, not reading. This is the "turn observers into participants" principle award sites use.

Pattern 03 - Type as a movie title

Words that rise into view

Award galleries in 2026 treat big typography like movie title screens - words animate as you scroll. Each word rises from behind an invisible line when the headline enters view. Restrained, editorial, and it makes text feel authored. (Scroll it into view to replay.)

Pattern 04 - The living pulse

A ticker that proves the platform breathes

A slow marquee of real, live activity - bookings, seats filling, weather calls. It signals the site is alive and others are trusting it right now (social proof + FOMO in one). Hover to pause. Feed this from real booking events in production.

Pattern 05 - Cursor spotlight

Cards that light up under your cursor

A soft amber glow follows the cursor inside each card. Subtle, premium, and it makes the surface feel physical and responsive - the page reacts to the person. Move your mouse across these.
